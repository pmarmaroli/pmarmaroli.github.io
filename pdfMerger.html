📄 PDF Merger

Merge PDFs and images into one PDF - 100% client-side

📁
Click to select PDFs/Images or drag & drop
Supports PDF, DOCX, JPG, PNG, HEIC and more
📋 Page order — drag to reorder
How it works: Select or drag multiple PDF files, Word documents (DOCX) and/or images (JPG, PNG, HEIC, etc.). DOCX files are rendered page by page; images are converted to PDF pages. They will be merged in the order shown. All processing happens in your browser — no files are uploaded to any server.
