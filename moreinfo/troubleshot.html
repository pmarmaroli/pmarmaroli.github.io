🔧 TroubleShot

🤖 AI-Powered DIY Solution Platform

TroubleShot revolutionizes DIY troubleshooting by leveraging advanced image analysis and AI to provide step-by-step instructions and tool recommendations. Users simply upload images of their problems and receive tailored, actionable solutions powered by OpenAI's GPT-4 vision capabilities.

Purpose: Democratizing DIY problem-solving through AI-powered visual analysis and multilingual support

🚀 Project Overview

This AI-powered troubleshooting platform combines advanced computer vision with natural language processing to create an intelligent DIY assistance system. Built with OpenAI's GPT-4 vision model and multilingual support, it provides users with comprehensive problem analysis and step-by-step solutions through simple image uploads.

💡Key Innovation: Computer vision-powered problem diagnosis with AI-generated solutions and multilingual accessibility for global users
