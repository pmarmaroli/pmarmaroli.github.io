TroubleShot

AI-powered DIY troubleshooting platform with computer vision and multilingual support

TroubleShot revolutionizes DIY troubleshooting by leveraging advanced image analysis and AI to provide step-by-step instructions and tool recommendations. Users simply upload images of their problems and receive tailored, actionable solutions powered by OpenAI's GPT-4 vision capabilities.
